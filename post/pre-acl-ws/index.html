CopeNLU is a Natural Language Processing research group led by Isabelle Augenstein and Pepa Atanasova with a focus on researching methods for tasks that require a deep understanding of language, as opposed to shallow pattern recognition. We are affiliated with the Natural Language Processing Section, as well as with the Pioneer Centre for AI, at the Department of Computer Science, University of Copenhagen.
